## Critical Trade-offs & Decisions

### 1. Compute: ECS vs Kubernetes

**Decision**: ECS Fargate  
**Trade-off**:

- âœ… Simpler ops, AWS-native, faster deployment
- âŒ Less portable, vendor lock-in
- **Priority**: Speed to market > portability

### 2. Database: PostgreSQL vs Document Store

**Decision**: PostgreSQL Aurora  
**Trade-off**:

- âœ… ACID compliance, complex queries, legal audit trails
- âŒ Higher cost, scaling complexity
- **Priority**: Data integrity > cost optimization

### 3. Search: PostgreSQL vs Elasticsearch

**Decision**: Hybrid approach  
**Trade-off**:

- âœ… PostgreSQL for simple queries, Elasticsearch for complex search
- âŒ Operational complexity, data sync overhead
- **Priority**: Performance > simplicity

### 4. Storage: S3 vs EBS

**Decision**: S3 with lifecycle policies  
**Trade-off**:

- âœ… Cost-effective, scalable, built-in redundancy
- âŒ Higher latency for frequent access
- **Priority**: Cost efficiency > performance
